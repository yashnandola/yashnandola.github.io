Nihar Prakash Gargava

Doctoral Student in Mathematics
École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland
School of Basic Sciences, Mathematics Section




Note: Until January 2023, I am a research intern at Quantinuum, at the Cambridge (UK) office.

I study number theory and its connections to lattices, error-correcting codes, sphere packings, quantum computing. My PhD advisor is Prof. Maryna Viazovska and I am a part of the Chair of Number Theory.

Before this, I was a masters student at EPFL and before that, a bachelor student at IIT Kanpur.

I write this wiki.
