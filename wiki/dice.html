D6

A D6 is a normal six sided die

Dividing the outcome

Suppose you roll 2 D6 and you want to partition the outcomes fairly

  • into two sets,
    • then it could be \(\{2,3,4,5,8,10\}\) and the complement,
    • or just checking if the sum is odd or even.
  • into three sets,
    • then it could be \(\{6,8,11\},\{3,7,9\},\{2,4,5,10,12\}\),
    • or just checking if the sum is \(0,1\) or \(2\) modulo \(3\).
  • into four sets,
    • then it could be \(\{5,6\},\{8,9\},\{2,3,7\},\{4,10,11,12\}\),
    • or just checking if the sum is \(0,1,2\) or \(3\) modulo \(4\).
