Laguerre polynomials

Contents

  • References

We have the definition

\[\begin{align} L_n^{(\alpha)} (x) = \sum_{i=0}^n (-1)^i {n+\alpha \choose n-i} \frac{x^i}{i!}. \end{align}\]

See [1].
