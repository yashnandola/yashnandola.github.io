Three point bounds

Contents

  • References

A class of upper bounds in optimization problems arising from considering interactions of triplets of points, instead of considering just pairwise interactions.

The following is a list of such bounds.

  • [1] concerns binary codes.
  • [2] is about spherical codes.
  • See here for sphere packing.
