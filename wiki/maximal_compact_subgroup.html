Maximal Compact Subgroup

Contents

  • References

Any connected Lie group \(G\) admits a maximal compact subgroup \(K\). It has the following properties.

  • Any compact subgroup of \(G\) containing \(K\) is equal to \(K\).
  • Any other subgroup of \(G\) with this property is conjugate to \(K\).

Any compact subgroup of \(G\) is contained in a maximal compact subgroup.
