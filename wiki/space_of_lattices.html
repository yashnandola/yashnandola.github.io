Space of Lattices

In \(\mathbb{R}^{n}\), the space of lattices is in a natural bijection with \(GL_n(\mathbb{R})/GL_n(\mathbb{Z})\). In particular, the space of lattices of covolume \(1\) is in bijection with \(SL_n(\mathbb{R})/SL_n(\mathbb{Z})\).
